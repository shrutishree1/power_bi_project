                                                                     **Superstore Sales Dashboard Summary Report**
---

==========================================================================================================

**Introduction** :
                  
              The superstore sales dashboard is a visual representation of sales data collected from a
              fictional store. Dashboard is created by using Power BI which aims to provide insights into
              various aspects of sales performance into three category products and the profit gain.
==========================================================================================================

**About the data**:

            - The data is downloaded from Kaggle for visualization purpose using Power BI. 
            - The data is all about the sales of different products which have been ordered online in United States. 
            - The data consists of products of 3 different categories with 17 different sub-categories sold in
              various states of United States in 4 different regions. 
            - The products were sold using 4 different shipping modes. The order date and shipping date is also available. 
            - The dataset also contains the profit gained and discount offered on these products. 
            - Total sales generated and total quantity ordered is also available.
                      • Row ID, Order Id, Customer ID, Product ID & Product Name
                      • Order Date: The date on which the order is placed.
                      • Ship Date: The date on which product was delivered to the customer.
                      • Ship Mode: We have 4 types of shipping mode- First Class, Second Class, Same day, Standard Class.
                      • Segment: Consumer, Corporate, Home Office
                      • Country: United States
                      • City & State: Different cities and states of United States.
                      • Region: East, West, South and Central.
                      • Category: Furniture, Office Supplies and Technology.
                      • Sub-Category: 17 different sub-categories.
                      • Sales: Total revenue generated from the product sales.
                      • Quantity: The total number of units sold.
                      • Discount: The percentage of discount applied to sales.
                      • Profit: The net profit obtained from sales after deducting costs.

==========================================================================================================

**Dashboard Overview**:
            • Cards presents a summarized view of total sales, net profit, average shipping days to
              delivered products and total ordered in total.
              It provides an overview of the overall performance of the superstore.
              
---

            • Sum of sales by segment indicates performance of all the segments in overall sales in
              which the consumer segment occupies 51% of total sales with corporate having 31%
              is next to it and then comes home office with 19% of total sales. Hover through the
              chart to see the total sales.
---

            • Sum of sales by region gives us region- wise sales distribution. In this we can see that
              we get more sales from west which is 32% of overall sales as compared to other
              regions. East is having 30% of sales, central is having 22% and south which is the least
              selling region with a contribution of 17% in total sales. Hover through the chart to
              see the total sales.

---

            • Sum of sales by state provides top 3 selling states, in which California is in top with
              total sales of 0.46M as compared to other states of United States. New York and
              Texas are 2nd and 3rd highest states by sales respectively.

---

            • There are two stacked area chart which shows the comparison of monthly sales and
              profit on year-by-year basis.

---

            • Sales by category and sub-category gives top 3 most selling categories and subcategories.

---

          • Sales by ship mode provides the insights of shipping preferences of customers. Most
            of the customers want standard class delivery which means customer are happy with
            standard delivery dates and less customer demands for same day delivery.

---

          • Through map, we can see total sales and profit generated in different states.

---

          • To see the above-mentioned details for a particular region, slicer helps in that case.

---

          • We also have a forecasting of sales by year and month. Forecasting of sales for 15
            days is there which has been analysed based on past sales record.
